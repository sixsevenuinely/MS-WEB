About Our Charity

Learn more about our mission, values, and the people behind our work.

Who We Are

Bedford MS Therapy Centre is a dedicated charity supporting individuals living with Multiple Sclerosis, Parkinson’s, and other neurological conditions. Our mission is to provide accessible therapies, emotional support, and a welcoming community environment for all who need us.

What We Do

We offer a wide range of services including physiotherapy, oxygen therapy, counselling, exercise classes, and social support sessions. Our centre is built on compassion, professionalism, and a commitment to improving quality of life.

Our Values

We believe in dignity, independence, and community. Every person who walks through our doors is treated with respect, empathy, and care.
